Entry 1: Limbo

Entry Date: Unknown Interval

As if it was one of the deeper pockets in my wallet, I had never seen such emptiness before. Surrounded by sparkling stars, waiting for one or two messages. Unknowning is the feeling it gave me; "who else could be here with me?". The few minutes I spent here felt like days, my thoughts being the only thing to keep me company.
